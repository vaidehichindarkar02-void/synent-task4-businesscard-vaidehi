# Task 4: Business Card Design

## Overview
This repository contains my submission for Task 4 of the Synent Graphic Design Internship. The objective of this task was to design a professional business card that represents a brand while maintaining a clean and visually appealing layout.

## Objective
The goal was to create a business card that presents important contact information in a clear and organized manner while following basic design principles such as alignment, spacing, and readability.

## Design Process
I first planned the overall layout and decided where each element should be placed. I included the logo, contact details, and other essential information while maintaining proper spacing and visual balance. I selected colors and fonts that matched the brand identity and ensured the final design looked professional.

## Tools Used
- Canva

## Files Included
- Front Business Card Design (PNG)
- Back Business Card Design (PNG)
- README.md

## What I Learned
This task improved my understanding of layout planning, typography, branding, and how to organize information effectively within a limited space. It also helped me create a professional and practical design.
